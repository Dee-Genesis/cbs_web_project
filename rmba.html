Application Details

Additional Considerations

01

Work Experience

While not mandatory, applicants with at least 2‚Äì3 years of professional or managerial experience are strongly encouraged to apply. Prior experience enhances classroom learning and peer collaboration within the cohort.

02

References

Applicants may be asked to provide academic or professional references to support their application. References should speak to the applicant's capabilities, character, and readiness for postgraduate study.

03

Statement of Purpose

A personal statement outlining career goals, motivation for pursuing the MBA, and preferred specialization may be required. This is an opportunity to demonstrate alignment with the program's objectives.

Credential & Recognition

Certification & Validation

Your MBA credential

MBA graduates receive a globally recognised master's-level credential, benchmarked against rigorous U.S. academic standards and validated through CIML Business School's extensive network of international academic partnerships.

  • MBA certificate issued by CIML Business School
  • Validated by California University FCE and benchmarked against U.S. standards
  • CIML Business School collaborates with government-accredited universities and institutions worldwide to ensure recognition and equivalency
  • Internationally recognised master's-level qualification accepted by employers and academic institutions globally
  • Serves as a gateway to doctoral-level study (DBA) and advanced executive leadership opportunities
