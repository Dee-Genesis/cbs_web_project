Who Can Apply

Eligibility Criteria

🎓
Academic Background
High School Diploma or GED (General Educational Development) certificate
Mature entry may be considered for applicants with relevant professional experience
🌎
Language Proficiency
Instruction is conducted in English; applicants must demonstrate English proficiency
Prior education completed in English
Standardized tests accepted: TOEFL or IELTS
Internal English assessment may be arranged where applicable
👤
Age Requirement
Minimum age of 18 years at the time of admission
📄
Application Requirements
Completed application form
Copy of High School Diploma, GED, or equivalent certificate
Valid government-issued photo identification
Proof of English proficiency (if applicable)
💡
Mature Entry Consideration Applicants who do not hold a formal High School Diploma or GED but have significant relevant professional experience may be considered for mature entry. Contact the Admissions Office to discuss your individual circumstances.

Credit Structure

Academic Information & Credit Units

Total Credit Hours

Industry-specific modules tailored to your chosen specialization pathway
📚
Study Load

Each module carries 3 credit hours. Full-time students typically complete 5–6 modules per semester, allowing for program completion within the standard duration. Part-time and blended study options are available for working professionals.

Your Credential

Certification & Academic Progression

Global Recognition

Diploma Certificate — Validated & Internationally Recognised

Upon successful completion, graduates receive a Diploma certificate from CIML Business School, validated by California University FCE and other government-accredited universities worldwide — ensuring your qualification carries global recognition and academic equivalency across institutions, employers, and government bodies.
