“The DBA is not the end of a journey — it is the beginning of a legacy. For those who lead at the highest level, applied doctoral scholarship is the instrument through which real change in business, policy, and society is driven.”

The Doctor of Business Administration at CIML Business School is a terminal professional doctorate designed for senior executives, experienced professionals, and advanced scholars who seek to integrate applied research with executive leadership practice. Candidates investigate complex organizational challenges, develop innovative solutions, and contribute original knowledge to both business theory and global professional practice.

Internationally benchmarked and validated, the DBA is structured to provide the highest level of expertise in strategic management, corporate governance, and industry-specific leadership — while equipping candidates with advanced research capabilities recognized across academic, corporate, and government institutions worldwide.

Key Program Features

Applied Doctoral Rigour, Real-World Impact

🔎

Applied Doctoral Research

Candidates conduct original research addressing real-world business challenges, producing dissertations that create direct organizational and industry impact — advancing both knowledge and professional practice.

🏗

Executive Leadership Development

Advanced training in board-level governance, strategic management, and organizational transformation at the highest tier — designed for those who lead, not those who aspire to lead.

🌎

Global Orientation

International case studies, comparative doctoral research methodologies, and cross-cultural leadership perspectives ensure graduates are equipped to influence business and policy across borders.

💼

Professional Relevance

Designed expressly for CEOs, senior managers, consultants, policymakers, and entrepreneurs — candidates are not required to leave their roles to pursue the DBA.

📚

Doctoral Research Mastery

Comprehensive training in advanced research methods, academic writing, and doctoral-level inquiry — building the investigative capacity required to contribute original knowledge to a global field.

🏆

Globally Validated Credential

DBA certificates are validated by California University FCE and other government-accredited universities worldwide, ensuring the qualification is recognized and equivalent across academic, government, and corporate institutions.
